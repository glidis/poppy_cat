有限理性的两个表现：

  • Excessive Extrapolation: expectting past multi-year trends to persist more than they do

  • Insufficient Adaptation: underreacting to recent news

两种收益率：

  • Simple Returns, \(P_{t+1}/P_t - 1\), 可跨资产相加，不可跨时间相加。
  • Compounded Returns, \(\ln(P_{t+1}/P_{t})\), 可跨时间相加，不可跨资产相加。
